Descriptive Style: Focuses on providing detailed descriptions of the restaurant's ambiance, decor, and overall atmosphere. It aims to paint a vivid picture of the dining experience.
Personal Narrative Style: Incorporates personal anecdotes and experiences from the visit to the restaurant. It adds a personal touch to the review and allows the reader to connect with the reviewer's perspective.
Analysis Style: Takes a more analytical approach by evaluating various aspects of the restaurant, such as food quality, service, cleanliness, and value for money.
Comparative Style: Compares the restaurant to similar establishments in terms of cuisine, price range, service quality, or other relevant factors. 
Gastronomic Style: Focuses primarily on the food and beverage offerings of the restaurant.
